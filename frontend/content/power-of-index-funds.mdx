Index funds are powerful because they turn investing into a repeatable behavior instead of a constant prediction exercise.

## Broad Exposure

A broad index fund spreads risk across many companies. Instead of betting on one winner, the investor participates in the growth of an entire market segment.

## Lower Friction

Lower fees, fewer decisions, and simple automation make it easier to stay invested during noisy periods.

## Long-Term Fit

Index funds work best when paired with patience, diversification, and a written plan that makes panic decisions less likely.
