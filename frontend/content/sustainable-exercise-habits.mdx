Building sustainable exercise habits starts with repeatability. The best plan is not the most intense plan; it is the plan that survives normal weeks, busy weeks, and low-energy weeks.

## Start Smaller Than Expected

Consistency improves when the first version of the habit is almost too easy. A short walk, a simple mobility session, or a basic strength routine creates momentum without forcing a full lifestyle overhaul.

## Track the Process

The useful metric is not only weight, distance, or personal records. Track whether you showed up, whether the routine felt recoverable, and whether it made the next workout easier to begin.

## Build Around Recovery

Recovery is part of the system. Sleep, nutrition, and planned deloads make the habit durable instead of fragile.
